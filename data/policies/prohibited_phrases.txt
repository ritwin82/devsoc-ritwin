PROHIBITED PHRASES IN CALLS

THREATS:
- "We will take legal action immediately"
- "Your credit score will be ruined"
- "We'll seize your assets"

PRESSURE TACTICS:
- "You must decide right now"
- "This is your last chance"
- "Don't tell anyone about this offer"

MISLEADING:
- "Zero fees" (when fees exist)
- "Guaranteed approval" (when not guaranteed)
- "Risk-free investment"

UNPROFESSIONAL:
- Any profanity
- Raised voice/shouting
- Dismissive language
